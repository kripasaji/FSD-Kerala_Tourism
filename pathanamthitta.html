Perunthenaruvi Falls

A natural waterfall situated on the banks of the River Pamba with a fall of 60 to 100 ft deep which pours down on a rocky bed.

Thriveni Sangamam

The point where the Holy Pamba River meets with the Manimala River in its north and with the Achankovil River in the south.
