Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The second largest wildlife sanctuary in Kerala and comprises of rare as well as endangered species of both flora and fauna.

Soochipara Falls

Also known as Sentinel Rock Waterfalls, Soochipara Falls is a three-tiered waterfall situated in Vellarimala, Wayanad. Surrounded by deciduous, evergreen and montane forests, this waterfall is regarded as one of the best waterfalls.
