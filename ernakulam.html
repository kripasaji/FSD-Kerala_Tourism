Willingdon Island

one of the largest human-made islands in India which is named after Lord Willingdon, the British Viceroy of India. This beautiful island provides various links between Kochi port and other national and international ports.

Fort Kochi

A seaside town in the south-western part of Main Kochi which is known for its old-world colonial charm.
